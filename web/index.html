Flix: Programming with Fixpoints

Flix (a backronym for FIXpoint Language) is a declarative, purely functional, strongly typed, and strongly normalizing programming language for fixpoint algorithms. The aim of Flix is to provide a domain specific language for modeling fixpoint problems and providing efficient solvers for such problems. Flix was originally conceived for declarative specification of static analyzers and is heavily inspired by Datalog.
